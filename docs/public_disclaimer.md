\# Public Disclaimer



This repository is an independent educational and analytical project based entirely on public data.



It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, approved by or reviewed by the Federal Reserve, any U.S. banking organization, any supervisory authority or any public agency.



\## Public-data scope



The project uses public sources only, including:



\- public Federal Reserve DFAST and stress test results;

\- public supervisory stress scenario files;

\- public Federal Reserve documentation;

\- public regulatory references;

\- public bank and macro-financial information where applicable.



No confidential supervisory information, confidential Federal Reserve models, confidential bank submissions, internal bank capital planning models, non-public supervisory data or non-public bank data are used.



\## Nature of the analysis



The project implements a reduced-form, public-data, DFAST-style stress testing replication for educational, benchmarking and reproducibility purposes.



It does not reproduce:



\- confidential Federal Reserve supervisory models;

\- official DFAST supervisory calculations;

\- official Stress Capital Buffer calculations;

\- internal bank stress testing models;

\- bank capital planning submissions;

\- legally binding supervisory determinations.



The results are approximate public benchmarks derived from observed public stress test outputs and public scenario information.



\## Interpretation limits



The results must not be interpreted as:



\- an official regulatory assessment;

\- a statement by the Federal Reserve or any supervisory authority;

\- an investment recommendation;

\- a credit opinion;

\- a bank rating;

\- a statement on the safety and soundness of any institution;

\- a prediction of future bank performance;

\- a substitute for supervisory judgment, internal risk management or regulatory capital planning.



\## Model risk



The project includes validation, robustness and model risk diagnostics. These diagnostics are designed to make limitations explicit.



A close fit to public Federal Reserve stress test outputs does not imply that the project has reproduced confidential supervisory models. It only indicates that a public reduced-form model can approximate selected published outcomes within the final modelling sample.



Remaining errors, tail deviations and threshold-sensitive cases should be interpreted as model risk.



\## Regulatory documentation



Regulatory documents included in the repository are used for interpretation, transparency and teaching. They should not be treated as a complete official archive.



PDF files were validated before publication. Files that failed PDF signature validation or did not open correctly were moved to a quarantine folder for auditability and should not be cited as valid regulatory documentation.



\## Responsible use



This repository is intended for teaching, public benchmarking, reproducible research and technical demonstration.



Users should independently verify data, assumptions, code, documentation and results before using the material in academic, policy, regulatory, investment or institutional contexts.
